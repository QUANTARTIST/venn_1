THE PRICE OF THE RIGHT TO BEAR ARMS

Debate around gun control often diverges toward a single issue - tighter gun control, solving mental health or societal issues or decreasing the prevalence of assault grade weapons. The following venn diagrams reveal America's deadly intersection.

2006 - 2018

Between 2006 and 2018 there were 68 mass shootings in the US.
In 33 cases the perpetrators had exhibited prior signs of mental health issues.
In 45 cases weapons were legally obtained.
In 47 cases assault grade weapons were used.
